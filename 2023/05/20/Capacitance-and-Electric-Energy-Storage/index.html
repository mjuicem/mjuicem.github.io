电容

  • 平行板电容

    $E = \frac{\sigma}{\varepsilon_0} = \frac{Q}{\varepsilon_0 A}$

    $V = Ed = \frac{Qd}{\varepsilon_0 A}$

    $C = \frac{Q}{V} = \varepsilon_0\frac{A}{d}$

  • 球形电容（内球面半径为a,外球面半径为b)

    $E = \frac{1}{4\pi \varepsilon_0} \frac{Q}{r} (a < r < b)$

    $V = \int_+ ^ -Edr = \int_a^bEdr = \frac{Q}{4\pi \varepsilon_0}(\frac{1}{a} - \frac{1}{b}) =\frac{Q}{4\pi \varepsilon_0}(\frac{b - a}{ab})$

    $C = \frac{Q}{V} =4\pi \varepsilon_0 \frac{ab}{b - a}$

  • 圆柱形电容

    选取一个圆柱形高斯面

    ​ $\because \oint EdA = E*2\pi r l = \frac{\lambda l}{\varepsilon_0} $ => $E = \frac{\lambda}{2\pi r \varepsilon_0}$

    ​ $\therefore \Delta V = \int_a ^bEdr = \frac{\lambda}{2\pi \varepsilon_0}ln(\frac{b}{a})$

    ​ $C = \frac{Q}{\Delta} = \frac{\lambda l}{\Delta V} = \frac{2\pi l \varepsilon_0}{ln(\frac{b}{a})}$

$\because C = \varepsilon_0 \frac{A}{d}$

$\Delta V = Ed$

$E = \frac{\sigma}{\varepsilon_0}$

$\therefore U = W_e = \frac{1}{2}C\Delta V^2 = \frac{1}{2}\varepsilon_0E^2(Ad)$

电介质

Definition：电阻率很大，导电能力很差的物质，即绝缘体

Feature：

  • 分子内部正负电荷束缚的很紧，介质内部几乎没有自由电荷
  • 外电场作用下，电子一般也只能相对于原子核有一微观的位移
  • 束缚电荷（induced bound charge）：不同于导体，电介质中的电子几乎是没有移动的，在介质表面产生的电荷称为极化电荷
  • 自由电荷(free charge)：导体中的自由电子

在平行电容板中：

$\kappa $ 是相对介电常数,附加电场为$E_0$,总电场为$E$,

$\because E = \frac{E_0}{\kappa}$，$E_0 = \frac{\sigma_0}{\varepsilon_0}$，$E_{ind} = \frac{\sigma_{ind}}{\varepsilon_0}$

$\therefore \sigma_{ind} = (1 - \frac{1}{\kappa})\sigma_0$

电容接电源

$\Delta V = \Delta V => E = E^‘ $

$Q^‘ = \kappa Q$ ,

$C^‘ = \frac{\kappa \varepsilon_0A}{d}$

电容不接电源

$Q = Q $

$\Delta V^‘ = \frac{\Delta V}{\kappa}$

$C^‘ = \frac{\kappa \varepsilon_0A}{d}$
